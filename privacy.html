SMessage — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Privacy Commitment: SMessage is built on the principle of zero data retention. We do not want your data, we do not ask for it, and our systems are designed to delete temporary session information automatically.

1. Information Collection & Technical Identifiers

SMessage is strictly a "no-logs" service. We do not collect, store, or transmit to our servers: PII (real names, phone numbers, or emails), permanent credentials, or chat history. Technical Security Disclosure: To comply with Google Play safety regulations and prevent service abuse, our system may process temporary technical identifiers, such as IP addresses. These are used solely for rate-limiting and enforcing blocks against users who violate our policies. These identifiers are transient and are not used to identify individuals.

2. End-to-End Encryption & Moderation

All messages are end-to-end encrypted (E2EE) directly on your device using keys derived from your 6-digit passcode.

  • Inability to Access: Because encryption happens locally, the developer cannot read, monitor, or intercept your chat content.
  • Moderation: As the developer cannot access unencrypted content, SMessage cannot verify reports of misuse or moderate the content of private rooms.

3. Temporary Session Data & Automatic Deletion

To facilitate real-time communication, our server processes minimal temporary data (Passcode Mapping and Socket Sessions). Data Deletion: All room data is wiped from the server's memory automatically after 40 minutes or upon room closure. Because we do not utilize user accounts, data deletion is instantaneous and automatic.

4. Local Storage Usage

To provide a seamless experience, we store small amounts of data locally on your device (Display Name, current room passcode, and terms acceptance). This data remains on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

5. Security & Limitation of Liability

While we utilize industry-standard AES encryption, no system is 100% secure. The developer is not liable for damages or unauthorized access resulting from the use of this service.

6. Third-Party Services

SMessage does not currently share data with third parties.

7. Children’s Privacy

You must be at least 13 years of age to use SMessage.
